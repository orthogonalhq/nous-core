The observed sidebar includes extra/non-reference sections and labels that make the screenshot read product-generic.

The rail is visible, but the drawer is absent and an input card appears where reference update cards/control treatment should dominate.

5. Surface layering remains less refined. `PAL-01` through `PAL-12` and `BT-07` are directionally present, but the screenshot reads as flatter medium-dark panels without the reference drawer glass/shadow layer.
6. Density and hierarchy are close but not fully reference-calibrated. `DIM-14`, `DIM-15`, `TYPE-06` through `TYPE-08`, and `BT-08` are serviceable in the canvas and updates list, but the overall screenshot has more empty center/right space because the drawer layer is absent.
